<p><span style="font-size: x-large;"><strong>Features</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Report crimes,bribes,disaters on Google Map</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Login feature through Google Account</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Search for places with autocomplete feature</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Option to toggle between Clustering based Map and Heatmap based Map</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Filter Crimes on Map among different types of crimes</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Details of latest reports within user's city</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">View Details of reports, comment on it with thumbs up/down option and flag wrong reports</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Details of user's past crimes and also crimes near user's location</span></p>
